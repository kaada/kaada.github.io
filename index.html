About

I'm a MSc in Computer Science student at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), where I specialize in Databases and Search. I'm currently writing a master's thesis on efficient processing of streams of high-frequency data.

Last summer I had an internship at TransferWise in London, where I interned as a full stack developer on the Borderless Account team, helping people without borders live global lives.

Prior to my internship in London, I was an exchange student at UC San Diego, where I took graduate classes in computer science, and undergraduate classes in economics and management.

Projects

Online Trading Bot

Created—and operated—a simple market-first high-frequency trading algorithm for the Steam Community Market. The bot executes a large amount of automatic trades, exploiting arbitrage opportunities in the market.

Terminal-based Music Player

Made a tool for playing cleartext music playlists, combining media files from different independent platforms. The program fetches the optimal media file, reducing battery and bandwidth usage, while increasing average bitrate and availability.
